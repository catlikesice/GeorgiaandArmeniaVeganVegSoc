City Spotlight · Batumi

Soak up Black Sea breezes while exploring Ajara’s coastal plant, fair trade, and organic cuisine.

Batumi’s conscious tables celebrate citrus groves, tea gardens, and seaside markets through sustainable sourcing. From sunrise smoothie bars to twilight wine terraces, your ethically-minded journey starts here.

See the featured table Browse the full list
  • Coastal bounty Chefs lean on mandarins, tea leaves, and mountain greens from Ajara.
  • Sunrise-to-midnight energy Beach cafés, night markets, and rooftop lounges keep the city humming.
  • Art-meets-food Expect gallery pop-ups, DJ sets, and seaside yoga pairings with menus.

Plan Your Visit

Mix beach time, tea fields, and vibrant markets into your Batumi itinerary.

Sunrise Ritual

Wake up with the sea and fuel your day with energizing plant plates.

  • Stretch on the New Boulevard boardwalk yoga deck.
  • Grab a smoothie bowl and cold brew at Black Sea Sprouts.
  • Cycle the coastal path to the Alphabetic Tower viewpoint.

Tea & Culture Day

Head inland for tea heritage and Ajara mountain hospitality.

  • Tour the Chakvi tea museum and pick your own leaves.
  • Lunch at Adjara Herb Kitchen with a guided tea pairing.
  • Return to the city for sunset tapas at Portside Pantry.

Insider Tips

Navigate Batumi’s vegetarian network like a local.

  • Reserve rooftop spots during summer festival weekends.
  • Ask about plant-based Adjaruli khachapuri specials.
  • Bring a reusable water bottle—many cafés offer citrus-infused refills.
