Invest in Impactful Partnerships

Our business membership program connects your team with regional advocates, policy leaders, and ethical supply chain partners who are expanding plant-based, fair trade, organic, and sustainable practices across Georgia and Armenia.

Choose the tier that matches the scale of your business and the depth of collaboration you want. Each option unlocks tailored support, visibility across our channels, and priority access to association initiatives.
