Experience Armenia’s capital through inventive, plant-centered cuisine.

Fresh mountain herbs, artisanal lavash, and slow-fermented produce anchor these kitchens. Discover the chefs redefining Yerevan’s dining scene with vibrant, sustainable menus.

Plan Your Visit

Blend market walks, coffee rituals, and cultural stops with your Yerevan dining itinerary.

Morning Market Circuit

Start at the Gum Market to sample dried fruit, wild mountain teas, and freshly baked gata.

  • Pick up sun-dried tomatoes and herb salts from local vendors.
  • Grab a plant-based lahmajun from the pop-up bakery.
  • Walk to Green Bean for a cold brew and mezze platter.

Art & Aperitifs

Spend the afternoon exploring galleries before a plant-forward dinner crawl.

  • Tour the Cafesjian Center for the Arts at the Cascade.
  • Sip herbal spritzers at Dalma Orchard Kitchen.
  • Sample natural wines and tapas at Drams Vegan Bistro.

Insider Tips

Make the most of Yerevan’s vegetarian network.

  • Reserve outdoor seating during apricot season for special menus.
  • Ask about Armenian-language cooking classes on Sundays.
  • Carry reusable containers—cafés offer discounts for bring-your-own jars.
