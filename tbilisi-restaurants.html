Editor's Pick

Livin Café Tbilisi

Set inside a light-filled courtyard in Vake, Livin Café layers Georgian vegetables with Levantine spices, house-fermented pickles, and cold-pressed juices. The team’s zero-waste pantry and relaxed service make it a refreshing pause between museum visits and park strolls.

Plan Your Visit

Blend your dining itinerary with markets, coffee breaks, and cultural stops.

Market Morning

Start your day at the Dezerter Bazaar with fresh fruit tastings and spice shopping.

  • Sample churchkhela made with walnut paste.
  • Pick up local tkemali sauces for souvenirs.
  • Walk to nearby Mama Terra for lunch specials.

Evening Crawl

String together an evening of rooftop views and garden patios.

  • Golden hour mocktails at Living Vino.
  • Dinner at Leila with live folk music.
  • Cap the night with raw desserts at Kiwi Café.

Insider Tips

Make the most of Tbilisi’s vegetarian network.

  • Call ahead for English-language cooking classes.
  • Ask about plant-based khinkali specials on weekends.
  • Carry reusable containers—many cafés offer discounts.
